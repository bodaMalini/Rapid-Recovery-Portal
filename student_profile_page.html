Lost

If you've lost an item, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Lost Item" button above.
  2. Fill out the form with details about your lost item.
  3. Upload a picture of the item (optional but recommended).
  4. Submit your listing, and it will appear on the Feed for others.

Found

If you've found an item, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Found Item" button above.
  2. Provide details about the item you found.
  3. Upload a picture of the item (optional).
  4. Submit your listing, and it will be displayed on the Feed.
