Lost

If you've lost an item, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Lost Item" button above.
  2. Fill out the form with details about your lost item, such as its name, description, and where it was last seen.
  3. Upload a picture of the item (optional but recommended).
  4. Submit your listing, and it will appear on the Feed for others to see.

Found

If you've found an item, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Found Item" button above.
  2. Provide details about the item you found, including its name, description, and where you found it.
  3. Upload a picture of the item (optional).
  4. Submit your listing, and it will be displayed on the Feed for others to identify.
